July 2012

Our paper titled “A crystal plasticity analysis of length-scale dependent internal stresses with image effects” has been accepted for publication in Journal of the Mechanics and Physics of Solids. Link

Our paper titled “Size-effects in textural strengthening of hierarchical magnesium nano-composites” has been accepted for publication in Materials Science and Engineering-A. Link

Our paper “Phenomenological crystal plasticity modeling and detailed micromechanical investigations of pure magnesium” appeared as one of the top 25 hottest articles in JMPS during Jan-March 2012. Link

May 2012

Shailendra has been appointed on the Editorial Board of Materials Science and Engineering: A (Publisher: Elsevier) as on May 25, 2012.

March-April 2012

Joshi Research Group is proud to announce its first PhD graduate. Ramin successfully defended his PhD thesis titled “ Modeling Slip Gradients and Internal Stresses in Crystalline Microstructures with Distributed Defects” on May 4th, 2012. He is officially Dr. Ramin Aghababaei now.

Abhilash’s paper “Stochastic Rate-dependent Elasticity and Failure of Soft Fibrous Networks” has been accepted for publication in Soft Matter. This paper is co-authored with Professor Prashant Purohit (MEAM UPenn).

We are pleased to organize a mini-symposium on “Plasticity: Bridging the Scales from Micro-Macro” at the 22nd International Workshop on Computational Mechanics of Materials to be held during September 24-26, 2012 in Baltimore, MD, USA. The MS is co-organized with Prof. Amit Acharya (CMU) and Prof. Jaafar El-Awady (JHU). Abstract submission deadline is May 15, 2012 and the submission website is here. We are listed as M.S.#13.

Jing’s paper “Phenomenological crystal plasticity modeling and detailed micromechanical investigations of pure magnesium” has appeared in Journal of the Mechanics and Physics of Solids.

Ramin’s paper “Nonlocal continuum crystal plasticity with internal residual stresses” appeared among top 25 hottest articles in Journal of the Mechanics and Physics of Solids in first quarter of 2011

Shailendra gave a talk titled “To Yield or Not to Yield: Materials Mechanics of the Ultra-soft and the Ultra-strong” at the bi-weekly Brown-bag ME Faculty Seminar Series. He is a co-organizer and coordinator of this internal seminar series with Profs. Nhan Phan-Thien and Vincent Tan.

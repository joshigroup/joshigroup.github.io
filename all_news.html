March-April 2012

Joshi Research Group is proud to announce its first PhD graduate. Ramin successfully defended his PhD thesis titled “ Modeling Slip Gradients and Internal Stresses in Crystalline Microstructures with Distributed Defects” on May 4th, 2012. He is officially Dr. Ramin Aghababaei now.

The MS is co-organized with Prof. Amit Acharya (CMU) and Prof. Jaafar El-Awady (JHU). Abstract submission deadline is May 15, 2012 and the submission website is here. We are listed as M.S.#13.
